About Me

Hello! I am a first year intending to study economics and data science. I aim to pursue a career in social entrepreneurship and venture capital, particularly in the field of education. I have experience in market research, entrepreneurship, and management through interning for a tech startup, curating for TEDx, publishing startup research, and leading a nonprofit I founded. On campus, I am also involved with Midway Ventures, Edge Entrepreneurship, House Council, and the Chicago Maroon. For recreation, I love to cycle, hike, paint, and play the piano.

My venture idea is a job search / internship platform that focuses on pro-bono work. A lot of companies, particularly startups, can offer meaningful work opportunities / projects for students, but they do not have the ability to pay students for their work. On the other hand, many students are looking for ways to obtain work experience. Oftentimes, they are willing to take advantage of meaningful work opportunities, even if they are unpaid. A platform that specifically focuses on pro-bono work can be made to connect these companies and students. Profit can be obtained through models like premium users (students pay a subscription to receive more opportunities). Current job search companies like Linkedin, Handshake, and Parker Dewey are not specialized in this type of work.
